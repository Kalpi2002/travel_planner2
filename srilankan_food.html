Sri Lankan Food

Discover the Flavors of Sri Lanka

Sri Lankan Rice and Curry

A traditional Sri Lankan meal consisting of rice served with a variety of curries and side dishes.

Hoppers

A popular Sri Lankan dish made from fermented rice flour and coconut milk, often served with an egg in the center.

Kottu Roti

A popular street food made from chopped roti, vegetables, eggs, and meat, stir-fried together with spices.
